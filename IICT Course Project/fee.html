The ultimate goal of faculty, comprising highly qualified professionals, at KIET is to ensure the provision of quality education that will enable the students for significant contributions both towards the development of their professional careers as well as the progress of their country.

The University is also governed by a Board of Trustees and Faculty Senate and supported by numerous offices and policies.
